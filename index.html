About Me

Turning data into decisions

Kia ora — I'm Felix, a Master of Business Information Systems student at the University of Canterbury with a passion for data, finance, AI, and business strategy. I enjoy turning complex problems into practical solutions through analytics, technology, and data-driven decision-making.

My professional background spans insurance, marketing, business operations, and retail management, giving me a strong understanding of how organisations create value and where technology can make a real impact. Alongside my studies, I have developed expertise in financial markets, winning both the University of Canterbury Trading Competition and the Tiger Brokers New Zealand Tertiary Student Sim-Trading Competition.

Through the UC Virtual Internship, I worked with Fuel50, a New Zealand HR technology company, conducting market research, competitor analysis, and evaluating future SaaS pricing strategies, including emerging AI-powered business models. I have also worked directly with New Zealand organisations through the Global Impact Industry Project and Global Leadership Programme, gaining valuable experience in client engagement, strategic problem-solving, and New Zealand business culture. Outside of work and study, I lead a multimedia team at my church, mentor fellow students, and enjoy building projects that combine AI, analytics, and business innovation.
